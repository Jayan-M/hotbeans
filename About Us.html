Image of our Employees, Amelia Carter, Lucas Reed, Sophia Nguyen, James Dimmock

Hot Beans Web started as a passion project by three friends with a shared dream, but it has grown into a thriving team of six innovators, led by a visionary CEO. Founded in 2018 by Amelia Carter (Design Visionary), Lucas Reed (Tech Wizard), and Sophia Nguyen (Marketing Maverick), the company began its journey in a small co-working space armed with little more than laptops, a whiteboard, and a relentless drive to make their mark in the web development world. 

 

 The founders’ mission was clear: to create more than just websites—they wanted to build digital experiences that empowered businesses to thrive. By blending Amelia’s design genius, Lucas’s coding mastery, and Sophia’s marketing expertise, they quickly gained a reputation for delivering outstanding results.

 

In 2024, James Dimmock joined Hot Beans Web as CEO, bringing fresh leadership and strategic direction to the team. With a proven track record in scaling creative tech companies, James has helped the company focus on sustainable growth while maintaining its innovative edge. Under his leadership, Hot Beans Web is expanding its reach, delivering bigger projects, and setting the stage for a new era of web development excellence.
